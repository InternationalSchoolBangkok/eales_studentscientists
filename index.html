Student Research and Publishing Program in Secondary School Science StudentScientists.org is committed to turning science students into student scientists. We have developed a program to achieve this, the Student Research and Publishing Program (SRPP). We have also developed a Model for Implementation of the SRPP as a guide for those interested in establishing the Program at their school. Learn more about the SRPP and how to implement it in your school.
